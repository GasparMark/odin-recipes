Juicy Chicken Roast

Description:

This roasted chicken recipe is the way my grandmother used

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Seasonings
  • Butter
  • Celery

Steps:

  1. Season chicken inside and out
  2. Add butter to chicken and pan
  3. Stuff the cavity with celery
  4. Bake until the chicken is fully roasted, 350 degrees F recomended
